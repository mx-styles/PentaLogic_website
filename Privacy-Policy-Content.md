## Section 1: Personal Data Ingestion & Collection

### Semantic Hierarchy: `<h2>` & `<h3>`
### SEO Keywords Targeted: `personal data collection, analytics metadata`

#### [Intro Text / Sub-headline]
> **We collect only the essential personal data necessary to provide high-fidelity consultancy services and maintain platform performance.**

#### [Ingestion Vectors]
*   ### Contact & Quote Form Variables
    When you request a consultation, we collect your Full Name, Business Email address, subject of inquiry, and detailed message parameters. This data is processed strictly to respond to business requests.
*   ### Professional Communications
    We retain records of professional emails, contact numbers, and project specifications sent to our corporate coordinates (`info@pentalogic.co.zw`).
*   ### Technical Analytics Metadata
    Our servers automatically log your IP address, browser footprint, operating system, and navigation paths across our sitemap layout to optimize browser loading times.

## Section 2: Data Retention & Security Frameworks

### Semantic Hierarchy: `<h2>`
### SEO Keywords Targeted: `data retention, database protection`

#### [Policy Declaration]
Pentalogic implements robust database protection protocols and restricts file access to authorized personnel to prevent unauthorized disclosure, leakage, or modification.

#### [Security Frameworks]
- **Encryption:** All transit data between your browser and our platform is encrypted utilizing SSL/TLS protocols.
- **Access Limits:** Access to client data is restricted using role-based authentication keys.
- **Retention Lifecycles:** Contact information and inquiry logs are retained for a maximum of 2 years following our last active communication, after which data is permanently scrubbed from our systems.

## Section 3: Regional User Rights Disclosures

### Semantic Hierarchy: `<h2>` & `<h3>`
### SEO Keywords Targeted: `user rights disclosures, regulatory opt-out`

#### [Regulatory Framework Compliance]
We respect regional privacy regulations and data protection guidelines, including the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) in South Africa, GDPR in Europe, and CCPA in North America.

#### [User Rights Index]
*   ### Right to Access & Rectification
    You have the right to request a digital transcript of all personal data Pentalogic holds about you and correct any inaccuracies.
*   ### Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)
    You can request that we permanently scrub your personal records and communication histories from our database logs.
*   ### Opt-Out & Consent Choices
    You have the right to opt-out of analytical tracking and cookies matrices at any time without degrading essential site performance.

## Section 4: Cookie Policy Matrix

### Semantic Hierarchy: `<h2>` & `<h3>`
### SEO Keywords Targeted: `cookie policy matrix, performance trackers`

#### [Tabular Index Definition]
We use cookies to analyze web traffic, remember preferences, and secure platform interfaces. Our cookie architecture is categorized as follows:

| Category | Purpose | Type | Lifespan |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
